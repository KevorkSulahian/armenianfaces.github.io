Armenian Writers

Why we did it

This is a website containing the biographies of some of the most famous and beloved Armenian literary figures, as well as analyses of a few of their works, as regarded by us.
The website was designed and created by six of us as a part of our final project for a course on Armenian Language & Literature. We took our skills of graphic and web design, and combined it with our love and respect for Armenian literature.
The students involved with this project are Kevork Sulahian, Nareg Soussani, Chougher Maria Doughramajian, Nanar Avedissian, Galin Dechoian, and Vani Mardirossian.
We hope you enjoy our take on Armenian literature and its most prominent contributors.
